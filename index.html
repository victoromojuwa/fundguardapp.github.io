🌍 About FundGuard

FundGuard Africa is a mission-driven fintech platform focused on safeguarding Africa’s digital ecosystem. Our app empowers users, banks, and fintechs to fight fraud, recover stolen funds, and verify financial identities.

  • 📁 Secure Case Management System

  • 🔗 Bank & Crypto Wallet Monitoring
