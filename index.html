About FundGuard

FundGuard Africa is a mission-driven fintech platform that empowers users, banks, and fintechs to fight financial crime through real-time monitoring, AI-powered alerts, case reporting, and recovery support.

Core Features

  • 🔍 Real-time Fraud Detection & Alerts
  • 📁 Secure Case Management System
  • 🛡️ Government & AML Verification Tools
  • 🔗 Bank & Crypto Wallet Monitoring
  • 📄 Document Analysis & Identity Validation
  • 💬 AI-powered Risk Messaging & Recovery Support
