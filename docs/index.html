About FundGuard

FundGuard Africa is an AI-powered fraud detection and fund recovery platform. We help individuals, businesses, banks, and fintechs monitor suspicious transactions in real time, track stolen funds across accounts and crypto wallets, and generate verified case reports for legal and regulatory action.

Core Features

  • 🔍 Real-time Fraud Detection & Alerts
  • 📁 Secure Case Management
  • 🛡️ AML/NDPR/CBN-aligned Compliance Tools
  • 🔗 Bank & Crypto Wallet Monitoring
  • 💬 AI-powered Risk Messaging
