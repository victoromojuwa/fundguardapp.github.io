About FundGuard

FundGuard Africa safeguards users, SMEs, and institutions from scams, theft, and cyber-enabled financial crime. We combine AI risk scoring, transaction monitoring, and compliant reporting so victims and partners can act fast — with traceability.

Core Features

Real-time Fraud Detection

AI risk models monitor accounts, wallets, and flows to flag abnormal behavior instantly.

  • Anomaly & velocity checks
  • Geo/IP pattern analysis
  • Self-serve alert center

Secure Case Management

Create a tamper-proof case with evidence, notes, and audit trails. Share read-only links with partners.

  • Case IDs • Chain of custody
  • Role-based access
  • Export legal-grade reports

Bank & Crypto Monitoring

Track flagged wallets, counterparties, and transaction paths across chains and accounts.

  • Wallet & IBAN watchlists
  • Heuristic clustering
  • Cross-platform signals

Compliance & Reporting

CBN/NDPR-aligned workflows with AML/KYC checks and EFCC-ready summaries.

  • KYC/AML screens
  • Regulator export packs
  • Data minimization
