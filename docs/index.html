Protecting Africa's Digital Economy from Fraud

About FundGuard Africa

FundGuard Africa is an AI-powered fraud detection and fund recovery platform committed to safeguarding Africa’s digital economy. We help individuals, businesses, banks, and fintechs monitor suspicious transactions in real-time, track stolen funds across accounts and crypto wallets, and provide verified case reports for legal and regulatory action.

Our mission is to empower Africans with tools to protect their assets, build trust in digital transactions, and contribute to a safer, more transparent economy.

Core Features

  • 🔍 Real-time Fraud Detection & Instant Alerts
  • 📁 Secure Case Management System
  • 🛡️ AML/NDPR/CBN-aligned Compliance Tools
  • 🔗 Bank & Cryptocurrency Wallet Monitoring
  • 💬 AI-powered Risk Messaging & Notifications
