About FundGuard

Africa’s first fraud detection & recovery platform. We combine fraud intelligence, fund tracking, and legal-grade evidence to help users, banks, and fintechs fight financial crime.

Core Value
  • Real-time fraud detection & alerts across channels
  • End-to-end recovery workflow with freeze requests
  • Evidence report generator for regulators & law enforcement
  • API for banks/fintechs to integrate monitoring & KYC
Presence & Expansion

Live in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, Uganda, Tanzania. Expansion road-map: Middle East → APAC → Europe → Americas.

Africa: 6 countries Middle East: 75% roadmap APAC: 60% roadmap
