🚨 Problem → ✅ Solution

Problem: Billions lost annually to mobile money scams, ATO, merchant fraud, and crypto theft. Users can’t trace funds; institutions see fragmented signals.

📉 $4B+ annual fraud impact (est. across Africa)
⏱ Hours–Days to respond using traditional processes
🧩 Fragmented tooling & reporting

FundGuard: End-to-end platform to detect fraud, track stolen funds, and recover with legal-grade evidence reports & freeze workflows — web, mobile, API.

🔍 Real-time anomaly flags & risk scores
💸 Fund tracking across banks, wallets, exchanges
📑 Evidence PDF + case audit trails
🛡️ Identity, AML & sanctions checks (roadmap to live integrations)
