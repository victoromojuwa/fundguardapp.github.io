The Problem & Opportunity

Digital fraud is silently draining Africa’s economy — from mobile money hijacks to crypto cash-outs to card fraud. Users rarely recover funds due to fragmented systems, slow escalation, and weak evidence trails.

  • Fragmented rails: banks, wallets, and exchanges don’t talk to each other fast enough.
  • No unified recovery: victims file reports but lack actionable, regulator-ready evidence.
  • High trust deficit: users and partners need verifiable, auditable incident workflows.

FundGuard closes the loop: detection → trace → evidence → recovery.

Product: Detection to Recovery

DetectionTrackingEvidenceAPI

  • Fraud Detection & Alerts — severity-based alerts with behavior analysis.
  • Fund Tracking — trace flows across banks, mobile money & crypto platforms.
  • Evidence Report Generator — timestamped, audit-ready reports for law enforcement & compliance.
  • Transaction Monitoring — anomalies, blacklist hits, and network mapping.
  • Predictive Risk Analytics — anticipate attempts before losses escalate.

How It Works

  • 1) User or partner reports case → intake creates a unique Case ID.
  • 2) Signals aggregated from bank/mobile/crypto sources (where integrated).
  • 3) Funds traced; high-risk routes flagged; freeze/notify actions prepared.
  • 4) Evidence report generated for regulators, banks & law enforcement.
  • 5) Recovery workflow proceeds with partners and legal processes.

Note: Some integrations are simulated in the MVP; production partners activate full data feeds.
