What is FundGuard?

FundGuard is an AI-powered platform that detects, tracks, and helps recover stolen funds across bank, mobile money, and crypto rails in real time.

Why Now

Digital fraud is accelerating across Africa. Victims and institutions need fast tools for detection, tracing, and case management—aligned with compliance and recovery workflows.

Key Features

  • Real-time fraud alerts
  • Wallet/account tracing
  • Secure case management
  • AML / sanctions checks
  • APIs for banks & fintechs

Market & Impact

Serving initial markets: Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa. Scaling to 20+ countries over 3–5 years.
Impact: supports SDG 8 & SDG 16 (inclusive growth & strong institutions).
